About

Welcome, my name is Dr. Tony Glover and I am the founder of DRT Software. I've been a software developer for over 25 years.

I use the web to do visualisation, simulation, virtual and augmented reality, learning environments, graphical tools and interfaces, as well as consultancy services.

I've visualised everything from brain activity to climate data and been privileged to work with a host of clients ranging from brain surgeons to artists to children with Aspergers syndrome.
