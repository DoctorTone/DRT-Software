About

Hi, my name is Dr Tony Glover and I am the founder of DRT Software.

I have over 25 years of experience in the software industry. I have worked in the commercial and academic sectors and have been involved in the fields of visualisation, simulation, virtual and augmented reality, medical imaging, animation, children's games and lots more.
