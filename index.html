About

Welcome!

I'm Dr Tony Glover the founder of DRT Software.

I build interactive and innovative graphical applications and have over 25 years experience in the software development industry. I specialise in data visualisation and also know a bit about virtual and augmented reality, simulation, graphical tools and a whole host of other things!
