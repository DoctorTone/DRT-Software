About

Welcome.

My name is Dr. Tony Glover and I am the founder of DRT Software. I have been a software developer for more than 25 years.

What do I do? I use the web to do visualisation, simulation, virtual and augmented reality, learning environments, graphical tools and interfaces. I've visualised everything from brain activity to climate data and been privileged to work with a host of clients ranging from brain surgeons to artists to children with Aspergers syndrome.
